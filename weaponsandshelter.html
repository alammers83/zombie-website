Weapons and tools

What kind of gun? A hand gun and rifle with a scope will work best for getting rid of zombies. A .22 will do for zombies when making headshots, no need to carry heavy ammo if you don't have to.

Your only foe won't, however, be zombies. You will be fighting off some survivors. You will need a shotgun for looters and crazies. It's a good idea to learn basic self-defense and hand to hand combat for both zombies and humans you will have to fight.

You will also need a quiet weapon. One that is effective, quiet and that you know how to wield. An axe, a machete, etc. If you can, make it carbon coated so it doesn't reflect light and draw attention. The less attention the better.

In a pinch, any blunt object will do though, just aim for the head and destroy the brain.

Shelter

Any place that has a minimal amount of entry and exit points on the ground level. If necessary, get to a second or third floor and destroy stairs with a sledge hammer. Ladders are your friend.

Avoid hospitals! This is where the infection will likely start and spread the quickest. Home is your best bet, but if you're in a city, get out. The less people around, the less zombies and you don't want to be where disease can spread easily.

A hammock tent if you must be out in the woods. Something you could possible place high up.

An RV would be ideal so you can sleep and keep on the move.

The most important thing is to know your entry and exit points and to fortify any weak spots with whatever you have available.
